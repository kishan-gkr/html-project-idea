Introduction

The Blood Donation & Donor Management System is an online platform that connects blood donors with patients and hospitals in need.
 It enables users to find nearby donors, request blood, and manage donation drives, ensuring faster and more efficient blood availability.

Objectives

Provide a centralized system to manage blood donors and requests.

Allow users to search for nearby donors or blood banks.

Enable hospitals to request blood for emergency cases.

Encourage voluntary blood donation through awareness and reminders.

Ensure quick response times for critical blood requirements.
