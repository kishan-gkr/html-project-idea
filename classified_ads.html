Classified Ads Website

Introduction

    The Classified Ads Website is a marketplace platform where users can post, buy, and sell products or services in different categories
    such as electronics, real estate, jobs, automobiles, and more. The system allows users to create listings, interact with buyers/sellers,
    and manage transactions securely.

Objectives

    - Provide a user-friendly platform for posting and browsing classified ads.
    - Enable secure communication between buyers and sellers.
    - Support multiple listing categories (jobs, rentals, services, etc.).
    - Offer premium ad promotions for better visibility.
    - Ensure moderation and fraud prevention through admin controls.
