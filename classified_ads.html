Classified Ads Website - Synopsis

1. Introduction

The Classified Ads Website is an online platform that allows users to post, browse, and manage advertisements across various categories such as real estate, jobs, vehicles, and services. It bridges buyers and sellers, offering an intuitive interface for listing products and services while ensuring secure interactions.

2. Objectives

  • Provide a dynamic platform for classified advertisements.
  • Enable users to search and filter ads by categories, location, and price.
  • Facilitate direct communication between buyers and sellers.
  • Ensure a secure and verified ad posting system.
  • Generate revenue through premium ad listings.
  • Maintain user engagement with a user-friendly interface.

3. Functionality

User Roles & Features

Users (Buyers & Sellers):

  • User Registration & Login
  • Post Ads with Images & Descriptions
  • Search, Filter, and Browse Ads
  • Contact Advertisers via Chat or Email
  • Mark Ads as Favorite
  • Manage & Edit Ads

Administrators:

  • Approve, Reject, or Remove Ads
  • Manage User Accounts & Permissions
  • Track User Activity & Generate Reports
  • Manage Featured & Paid Listings
  • Ensure Content Moderation & Ad Verification

Premium Features (Optional):

  • Featured Ads for Paid Promotions
  • Subscription Plans for Businesses
  • Ad Expiry & Auto-Renewal Options

4. Tech Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Bootstrap for UI)
  • Backend: PHP (Laravel or Core PHP)
  • Database: MySQL
  • Server: Apache or Nginx
  • Additional Technologies: AJAX, jQuery, Payment Gateway

5. Who Can Use This System?

  • General Users: Individuals or businesses posting and browsing ads.
  • Businesses: Companies promoting their products or services.
  • Administrators: System managers overseeing content moderation.
