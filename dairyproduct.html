Introduction

This project is an online milk product selling platform that helps local dairy businesses sell their products directly to customers. It includes features like product listing, order management, online payments, and delivery tracking.

Objectives


Provide a simple and efficient e-commerce solution for milk and dairy product sellers.

Allow customers to browse, order, and pay for dairy products online.

Offer a subscription-based delivery system for daily milk needs.

Enable sellers to track orders, manage inventory, and analyze sales reports.
