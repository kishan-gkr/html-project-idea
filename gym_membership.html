Gym Membership & Workout Planner System

Introduction

    The Gym Membership & Workout Planner System is a digital platform that allows gym owners to manage memberships,
    track payments, and monitor member progress, while enabling gym members to schedule workouts, track progress, 
    and receive personalized fitness plans.

Objectives

    - Provide seamless membership management for gyms and fitness centers.
    - Offer personalized workout plans based on user goals and fitness level.
    - Allow members to track workouts, diet plans, and progress.
    - Enable trainers to assign and monitor workouts for members.
    - Automate billing, attendance tracking, and class scheduling.

Project Functionality

For Gym Members:
    ✅ Register & Subscribe: Sign up and choose a membership plan.
    ✅ Access Workout Plans: Get personalized workout routines from trainers.
    ✅ Track Progress: Log weight, reps, and calories burned.
    ✅ Schedule Personal Training: Book 1-on-1 training sessions with trainers.
    ✅ View Diet & Nutrition Plans: Get AI-generated meal suggestions.
    ✅ Receive Workout Reminders: Get notifications for daily workouts.
    ✅ Join Live or Recorded Workouts: Participate in virtual training sessions.
For Gym Trainers & Coaches:
    ✅ Create & Assign Workout Plans: Customize workouts for each member.
    ✅ Monitor Member Progress: Track fitness improvements and milestones.
    ✅ Manage Class Schedules: Organize group workouts and boot camps.
    ✅ Send Fitness Tips & Updates: Notify members about fitness challenges and new exercises.
For Gym Owners & Admins:
    ✅ Manage Gym Memberships: Track active members, renewals, and expirations
